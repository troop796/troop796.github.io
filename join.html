How do I join Troop 796?

Youth can join Scouts BSA if they have completed the fifth grade and are at least 10 years old, OR have earned the Arrow of Light Award and are at least 10 years old, OR are age 11 but have not reached age 18.

Online:
Go to my.scouting.org and create an account. After creating an account, select Troop 796 St Ignatius Of Loyola Catholic Church. Here is our BeAScout page

Paper:
Complete and submit a BSA youth application, to the Committee Chair, or Scoutmaster, through the troop email or in person.

  • Include BSA membership number, if applicable
  • Parents must sign and date form
Complete and submit a BSA health form, to the Committee Chair, or Scoutmaster, through the troop email or in person.
  • Parts A and B are required to participate in troop activities and local camp outs
  • Parts A, B, and C are required before attending summer camp, including a physical form from a doctor
